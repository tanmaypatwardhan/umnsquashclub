Squash is racket sport typically played by two players on a
four walled court with a rubber ball. Players take turns
hitting the ball on the front wall, and each player has
to return the ball within two bounces. While the game's rules
are fairly simple, a standard rally has lot of strategy involved
that forces the player to think about factors like movement,
shot placement, and their opponents' weaknesses.
